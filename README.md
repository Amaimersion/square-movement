# Square movement

Square movement of square matrix.

## Examples

### № 1

Initial:
```
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9
```

One step:
```
4 1 2
7 5 3
8 9 6
```

Two steps:
```
7 4 1
8 5 2
9 6 3
```

etc.

### № 2

Initial:
```
1 2 3 4
5 6 7 8
9 1 2 3
4 5 6 7
```

One step:
```
5 1 2 3
9 1 6 4
4 2 7 8
5 6 7 3
```

Two steps:
```
9 5 1 2
4 2 1 3
5 7 6 4
6 7 3 8
```

etc.
